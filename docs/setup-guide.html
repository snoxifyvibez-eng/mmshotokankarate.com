What we need to switch the website fully on

The website is built and ready. To finish the job, we need to connect a few outside services — the ones that send your enquiry emails and take payments. Most are free; Stripe (for payments) needs your business and bank details, which only you can provide. This guide walks through each one in plain language and tells you exactly what to send us.

02

Step by step

A · Web3Forms — enquiry & registration emails

Free

Whenever someone fills the contact or registration form, this delivers it straight to your Gmail. No software to install.

  1. Go to web3forms.com.
  2. Enter your dojo email: mms.shotokan.karate@gmail.com.
  3. They email you an Access Key (a short code). Copy it.
Send usThe Access Key from your email. That’s all — we plug it in.

B · Stripe — online payments

Per-payment fee

Stripe securely handles the one-time registration fee and the monthly membership. Card details go straight to Stripe — they never touch your website. Money lands in your bank account.

  1. Create a free account at stripe.com using the dojo email.
  2. Complete business verification — Stripe asks for the dojo/owner details and a piece of ID. (Required by law before you can accept real payments.)
  3. Add the bank account where you want payouts deposited.
  4. Decide your prices, e.g. Registration $[50] one-time and Membership $[80]/month. Tell us the amounts — we’ll set up the matching products, or you can create them and we’ll connect them.
  5. Invite us to your Stripe account as a team member (Stripe → Settings → Team), or share the API keys with us securely (we’ll tell you how).
Send us (securely — never by plain email) Either a team invite to your Stripe account, or your Secret key (sk_live_…) and the two Price IDs (price_…). We’ll guide you through a safe way to share these.

C · Hostinger — hosting & web address

Already set up

Your site lives here. We already have the FTP access to publish updates. Two quick confirmations from you:

  1. Make sure the free SSL certificate is switched on (the padlock / https) — in hPanel under SSL.
  2. Confirm the site uses PHP 8 — hPanel → PHP Configuration. (We’ll double-check too.)
Send usNothing new needed — just let us know the domain name we should publish to if it isn’t mmshotokankarate.com.

D · Dojo inbox

Already have

All enquiries, registrations, and payment alerts arrive at mms.shotokan.karate@gmail.com. Just keep an eye on it (and the spam folder for the first few) after launch.

Once we have the items above, we connect everything, run a full test payment in Stripe’s safe “test mode”, switch it to live, and publish. Typical turnaround is 2–3 business days after we receive your details. We’ll confirm each step with you before going live.
